About this course

i made this course simply as i feel that most toki pona courses do not focus enough on context. as toki pona is a heavily context-dependent language, ignoring the need to teach context often leads to situations like this:

would it be appropriate to make a Toki Pona phrase for the genre "shoegaze" that would be "lukin len noka"? or should you just pronounce it like "sukes"
alternatively, not teaching the minimalism aspect of toki pona well leads to situations like this:
I want to use as much words as possible (without it sounding unnatural) to say...
both of those quotes come from conversations i've been in
